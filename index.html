About Iqra

Welcome to your Hifz Journey!

What is Iqra?
Iqra is your personal digital companion designed to make memorizing the Quran organized, focused, and rewarding.

How to use your portal:

  • Dashboard: Check your daily attendance streaks, see your overall progress bar, and view any specific verses your teacher wants you to revise.
  • Hifz Calculator: Type in how many pages you learn a day to find out the exact date you will finish memorizing the entire Quran.
  • Study Focus Hub: Read clear Arabic text, listen to beautiful audio recitations verse by verse, and use the built-in timers to break your study sessions into easy chunks (like Sabak and Dhor).
  • Milestones: Earn beautiful glowing badges when you complete a full Para or have a flawless recitation with no mistakes!

Keep going! Your dedication to the Quran brings immense rewards in this life and the next.

Welcome to the Tutor Portal

What is Iqra?
Iqra is an advanced management platform built to help teachers accurately track, evaluate, and guide their students through the Quran memorization process without relying on paper logs.

How to manage your classroom:

  • Enroll Students: Use the top right button on the dashboard to securely add new students.
  • Manage Progress (Sabaq, Sabqi, Manzil): Use the dedicated progress tab to set each student's current Juz, explicitly detail their lessons, and securely remove old student profiles if needed.
  • Log Recitation Data: Rate fluency (1 to 5 stars), log mistakes, and write specific Tajweed notes that the student can view.
